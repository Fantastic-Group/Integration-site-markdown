### Once you sign NDA and send to your ACT Account Manager
 
After the NDA is signed your ACT Integration Team will send you a welcome email with credentials, allowing you to access the ACT Integration Portal.   This contains all the integration documentation and API Playground required to complete the integration.
 
To sign up for a Sandbox Account, you must complete the request form on the ACT Integration Portal.
 
#### Basic Client Information
 
- Business Name
 
- Description of Business
 
- Name of Licensee
 
- License Number
 
- Owner
 
- Owner Contact Information
 
- Other Brand Names
 
- Office Address
 
- Office Phone
 
 
#### Platform Provider Information
 
- Office Address
 
- Office Phone
 
- Technical Contact
 
- Technical Contact Phone
 
- Jurisdiction Information
 
- Details of your jurisdiction
 
- Details of who needs access to the ACT Sandbox Environment
 
- First name
 
- Surname
 
- Email Address
 
- Address
 
- Role
 
This information is sent to your ACT Integration Team, your Sandbox Account details will be emailed to you within 72 hours.  
 
## Start 2-week integration supported by ACT Integration Team
 
We now enter a two-week integration period.
 
You will be sent details of the SLACK channel that has been created specifically for communication through the 2-week integration. 

 
### Support Plan
 
During the ACT Geosafe Integration process technical support will be provided.  The goal is to ensure a seamless integration process to resolve questions efficiently and in a timely manner.  
 
Integration Support Structure
 
Level 1 Support (ACT Integration Team) 
Method of contact (JIRA tickets, email, Slack)
 
### Integration complete Customer is booked in for a pre-live certification with Fantastic Tech
 
The pre-live certification is a meeting coordinated by the ACT Account Manager. During this session, the Tech team will conduct the necessary tests and checks to confirm a successful integration. A certification test plan will be provided and completed as part of this process.
 
### Production Key is sent to Customer
 
Once the certification process is successfully completed, we will create your account in the Production database and issue your Production API key.
